mercurr

Cofounder

mercurr is an online marketplace connecting growing companies expanding abroad with a selected network of local professionals. We worked closely with a community of 300+ recruiters, digital marketing & PR agencies, lawyers & accountants and sales professionals in more than 15 countries. So far, we've helped 30+ companies, such as Parrot or Withings, connecting with the right partners.

I am a cofounder of mercurr. I was in charge of building our network of partners and getting them onboard, on the providers side of our marketplace. I set the whole sales strategy, took care of the legal part, manage their profile and ended building a great community of partners.

W Project

Cofounder

W Project is a Web Media dedicated to French Entrepreneurship who set up their company in a foreign country. Young grad teams travel the world, meet and interview entrepreneurs during their trip. They then publish videos, share their stories, experiences and advice about setting a company abroad. So far, 15+ people travelled the world thanks to the W Project, publishing 350+ videos on our website.

I cofounded this organisation when I graduated and spent one year travelling 13 countries and interviewing 80+ entrepreneurs. When we came back home, we decided to take the project to the next level and managed to find new teams to run it. I am still involved in the strategy.

French Tech HK

Coordinator

La French Tech is a community of French people working in the Tech industry, all around the world. This community is organised around cities and hubs, nominated by a dedicated organisation. Hong Kong was recognized as a 'French Tech Hub' beginning of 2016. I've been part of the operational team ever since: building the website, organising events and helping companies to get access to a quality network in Hong Kong.
